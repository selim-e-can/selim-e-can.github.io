Path Following Car

I used sensor fusion and PID control to program a path following car.

Summary

A TI-RSLK car equipped with 8 Infrared Sensors (phototransistors) was used to create a path following car. The black track reflects IR light and white parts of the track absorb it (Note: this is the opposite with visible light). Thus, when the car is centered, the sensors in the middle give higher readings than those outside the center of the car. Using this property, we can build a PD (Proprtional-Derivative) control feedback system that corrects the car as it encounters turns or crosspieces. me and my partner implemented this feedback system and programmed the car to do a donut/U-turn as can be seen at the end of video 1.
